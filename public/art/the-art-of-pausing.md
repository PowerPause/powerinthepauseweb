# The Art of Pausing® | Power In The Pause®

A creative alternative to meditation and journalling. And an iPhone app.

## A different way to pause

The Art of Pausing® uses simple pen-and-paper creative practices to help you step out of constant thinking, create space and reconnect with yourself. You do not need artistic experience, the right words or a perfectly quiet mind.

People who find it hard to sit still to meditate tend to find this works when nothing else has. Your hands are busy with a simple creative practice, so your mind can settle.

## You do not need to be an artist

This is not an art class, and there is no right way for the finished page to look. A pen, a sheet of paper and a willingness to begin are enough.

## Meet yourself where you are

The Art of Pausing® begins with what feels present, whether that is grounded, hopeful, joyful, restless, overwhelmed, stuck or something in between. The app currently presents twelve emotional pathways.

## Inside the app

- **The Pause Portal** — a guided creative practice that moves your attention from your thoughts onto the page.
- **Personal Reflections** — gentle reflections shaped by what you noticed and created.
- **Your Journey** — your drawings, emotional landscape and reflections gathered quietly in one place.
- **The Field** — an anonymous gallery of drawings shared by people pausing around the world, with no likes and no comments.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need to be good at art?**
No. There is no right way for the finished page to look.

**How long does a practice take?**
Most Pause Portal experiences take around thirty minutes. Spiral Shift®, the free introduction, takes about five minutes.

**What materials do I need?**
Just basic materials: a pen and a piece of paper.

**How is this different from meditation?**
Rather than asking you to empty your mind, The Art of Pausing® gives your attention somewhere to go, so your mind can settle.

**Is this therapy?**
No. It is a creative wellbeing practice, not a substitute for therapy or medical care.

**Which devices does the app support?**
The Art of Pausing® app is launching first on iPhone.
