# Retreats | Power In The Pause®

Power In The Pause® retreats, guest facilitation and creative experiences.

A retreat offers something everyday life rarely gives us: enough space to step out of familiar patterns, reconnect with ourselves and notice what is ready to shift. Katie Cooper hosts her own retreats and also brings The Art of Pausing® into retreats led by others. No artistic experience is needed.

## Join a Power In The Pause® Retreat

My own retreats are created for people who want to step away from the usual pace of life and reconnect with what matters, built around the central philosophy: pause creates space, space creates awareness, awareness creates change.

When no retreat is currently open for booking, [register your interest](https://thepowerinthepause.co.uk/contact/) to hear about future Power In The Pause® day retreats and residential experiences.

## Invite Katie to Your Retreat

Katie also brings The Art of Pausing® into retreats hosted by other people, as a standalone workshop or woven into a wider programme. These sessions work particularly well within wellbeing retreats, personal-development retreats, leadership and business retreats, creative retreats, women's retreats, team away days, private groups and communities, and bespoke events and gatherings.

## What a Session Can Include

A guided creative practice using simple pen-and-paper materials, individual reflection, optional shared conversation, creative exploration of an emotion or theme, gratitude, intention or transition practices, space to process what has emerged, and practical ways to continue the pause beyond the retreat.

## Why Creative Practice?

Creative practice gives people another route into reflection. When attention moves from analysing to making marks on a page, thoughts can soften, patterns can become easier to notice and new perspectives can emerge. The drawing is not the outcome. The shift it creates is the outcome.

## Frequently asked questions

**Do I need to be artistic?**
No. The practices are designed for people with all levels of creative experience. The emphasis is on reflection and experience, not artistic ability.

**How long is a session?**
Sessions can range from approximately 45 minutes to half a day, depending on the theme, group and wider retreat programme.

**Do you travel?**
Yes, subject to availability, travel and accommodation arrangements.

**Do you host your own retreats?**
Yes. Future Power In The Pause® retreat dates will be shared on this page and through the interest list.
